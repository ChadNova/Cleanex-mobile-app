# CleanEx App Update

## Overview
This update expands the **CleanEx mobile application** by introducing additional home-care services. CleanEx continues its mission of providing fast, reliable, and professional cleaning services while creating sustainable job opportunities for women service providers.

The new update adds **Carpet Deep Cleaning** and **Bed Covers Cleaning** services to improve the platform’s service offerings and meet growing customer needs.

---

## New Services

### 1. Carpet Deep Cleaning
Professional carpet deep cleaning is now available through the CleanEx app. This service helps remove dirt, stains, dust, and allergens from carpets using professional cleaning techniques.

**Features**
- Deep stain and dirt removal
- Odor elimination
- Improved indoor hygiene
- Suitable for homes, offices, and apartments

**Use Cases**
- Residential homes
- Offices
- Student housing
- Commercial spaces

---

### 2. Bed Covers Cleaning
CleanEx now provides professional cleaning for large bedding items and bed covers.

**Includes**
- Bed sheets
- Duvet covers
- Blankets
- Pillow covers
- Large bed covers

This service ensures bedding is properly washed and handled by trained CleanEx workers.

---

## How It Works
1. Open the **CleanEx mobile application**
2. Navigate to the **Services** section
3. Select one of the new services:
   - Carpet Deep Cleaning
   - Bed Covers Cleaning
4. Choose a preferred date and time
5. Confirm the booking
