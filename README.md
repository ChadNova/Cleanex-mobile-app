# CleanEx Mobile App

## Overview
**CleanEx** is a mobile platform designed to connect busy people—especially students and young professionals in Kigali—with trusted local cleaners for essential home services.

Many people struggle to balance work or academic life with everyday tasks such as laundry and home cleaning. At the same time, many skilled women remain unemployed despite being willing and able to work.

CleanEx addresses this gap by using technology to connect people who need household services with women looking for flexible job opportunities. The platform focuses on **convenience, transparency, and job creation**.

---

## Services

CleanEx currently provides the following services:

### Laundry
Users can request professional laundry services with convenient pickup and delivery options.

### Shoe Cleaning
Professional cleaning services for sneakers, leather shoes, and other footwear.

### Home Cleaning
Trusted cleaners provide household cleaning services for apartments, homes, and student housing.

### Carpet Deep Cleaning *(New)*
Professional carpet cleaning that removes deep dirt, stains, dust, and allergens.

**Features**
- Deep stain and dirt removal  
- Odor elimination  
- Improved indoor hygiene  
- Suitable for homes, offices, and apartments  

### Bed Covers Cleaning *(New)*
Specialized cleaning for large bedding items.

**Includes**
- Bed sheets  
- Duvet covers  
- Blankets  
- Pillow covers  
- Large bed covers

## How It Works

1. Open the **CleanEx mobile application**
2. Navigate to the **Services** section
3. Choose the service you need
4. Select a **date and time**
5. Confirm your booking

Users can easily manage their bookings and receive confirmation instantly.
